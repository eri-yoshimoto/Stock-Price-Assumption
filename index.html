📄 Google Apps Script Setup

Follow these steps once to enable automatic logging to your Google Sheets tracking file.
  1. Open your Stock Price Tracking Google Sheet.
  2. Click Extensions → Apps Script.
  3. Replace the entire editor content with the script below.
  4. Click Deploy → New Deployment → Web app.
  5. Set "Execute as" to Me and "Who has access" to Anyone.
  6. Click Deploy, copy the Web App URL, and paste it in Settings above.
